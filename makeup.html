The BEST Of 2021 MakeUp

We've got you covered
Jennifer Hussein
5 February 2022

THE AUTUMN ISSUE

FOUNDATION

If your jaw didn't drop to the floor watching the hundreds of swatches from TikTok users who were lucky enough to get their hands on this L'Oreal Paris Infallible 24HR Fresh Wear Foundation In A Powder compact, you must not have watched the right videos. It was the peak of the pandemic, February 2021, to be exact, when beauty TikTok found out about L'Oreal Paris's newest foundation drop, and boy did the secret come out. Of course, Allure was anxious to review this product, but it took almost two months for one of our editors to finally get a compact to try, as it was sold out everywhere — and I mean everywhere. Making pores disappear, covering discoloration, redness, and even acne with just one swipe, this medium-to-full-coverage foundation isn't the least bit cakey and gives a filter-like effect. Powder foundation not your thing? Don't worry, there's a L'Oreal Paris Infallible Fresh Wear 24HR Foundation In A Liquid that also has TikTok's stamp of approval (as well as ours).

CONCEALER

The Nars Soft Matte Complete Concealer is full coverage and we'll argue that it deserves just as much hype as its counterpart, the Radiant Creamy Concealer. The Soft Matte version is basically magic in a little pot. Available in 30 shades, the cream formula blends and blurs beautifully into skin, leaving you with — as the name suggests — a high-coverage, soft-matte finish. Plus, a little goes a long way; the lightest tap into the concealer pot picks up the perfect amount of product. Lennie uses the high-pigment base before applying foundation to cover any skin-care concerns.

LIPSTICK

Glossier boasts that its Ultralip range is "the cashmere sweatpants of lipsticks" and, well, it's not wrong. With a juicy blend of watermelon extract, jojoba oil, and more, this formula provides a slick feel with just enough color to be noticeable. Summer begs bright colors, which is why we'd roll with the shade Fête, a pink-toned red.

MASCARA

Makeup artist Lavonne has fallen head over heels for the mega-viral Maybelline Lash Sensational Sky High Waterproof Mascara in Very Black — so much that when she spotted a rare restock in person, she grabbed three tubes in one go. "This mascara makes my lashes look like I've had a lash lift and use growth serums," she says of the drugstore volumizing wonder. For $11, those results are hard to beat.

BLUSH

Rare Beauty's Stay Vulnerable Melting Cream Blush gives off a romantic feel from the very first swipe. The liquid-like, water-resistant formula feels like a second skin and won't cake, crease, or clog pores. It's spiked with a nourishing blend of meadowfoam and sunflower seed oils, as well as lotus seed, to leave skin utterly glowing. "These blushes didn't come to play any games," editorial assistant Gabi Thorne confirms. "They leave a healthy dose of pigment on the cheeks" on her brown skin, she adds.
